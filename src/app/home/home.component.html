Why skiing in Serbia?

In Serbia (Srbija), you can look forward to 97 kilometres of slopes: the ski resorts are served by 85 ski lifts. Have lots of fun skiing in Serbia (Srbija)! In the list of the best ski resorts in Serbia (Srbija), the ski resort Kopaonik is top with 3.3 out of 5 stars. The largest ski resorts offer up to 55 kilometres of slopes (Kopaonik). The highest ski resorts for skiing in Serbia (Srbija) extend up to an altitude of 2,017 metres (Kopaonik). And our tip for the best value for money is the ski resort Tornik - Zlatibor.
